# Competitor Research

## Suno AI
- Free: $0/mo — 50 credits/day (10 songs), v4.5 only, no commercial use, standard features only, 1 min audio upload, shared queue, no add-on credits
- Pro: $8/mo (yearly) or $10/mo (monthly) — 2,500 credits/mo (500 songs), v5 model, commercial use, pro features (personas, advanced editing), 12 stem splits, 8 min audio upload, add vocals/instrumentals to existing songs, priority queue (10 concurrent), add-on credits available
- Premier: $24/mo (yearly) or $30/mo (monthly) — 10,000 credits/mo (2,000 songs), Suno Studio access, all Pro features
- Key features: Remix, cover, extend, co-write, personas (consistent voice), magic song descriptions, basic/advanced editing, stem extraction, upload audio

## Udio AI
- Free: $0/mo — 10 credits/day, 100 credits/mo (no rollovers), 4 concurrent songs, limit 3 full-length (2:10s) per day
- Standard: $8/mo (yearly) or $10/mo — 2,400 credits/mo, 6 concurrent songs, voice control, editing, audio upload, style reference, custom cover art
- Pro: $24/mo (yearly) or $30/mo — 6,000 credits/mo, 10 concurrent songs, all features
- Add-on credits: 100 for $3, 1,000 for $25
- Key features: Voice control, remix, extend, style reference, edit music/lyrics, cover art, blend/reduce styles, premium artist styles

## AIVA
- Free: €0 — 3 downloads/mo, no commercial use, copyright owned by AIVA
- Standard: €11/mo (annual) — 15 downloads/mo, copyright owned by user, monetization on YouTube/Twitch/TikTok
- Pro: €33/mo (annual) — 300 downloads/mo, full copyright, monetization everywhere, WAV/MIDI/stems
- Key features: Classical/cinematic focus, MIDI export, influence from existing compositions, style presets, orchestral arrangements

## Soundraw
- Creator: $11.04/mo (annual) or $19.99/mo — unlimited songs, 50 downloads/mo, personal/commercial use
- Artist Starter: ~$32/mo — 10 downloads/mo with simple license, monetization
- Artist Unlimited: ~$32.49/mo — unlimited downloads, MP3/WAV/stems
- Key features: Royalty-free, customizable length/tempo/instruments, mood-based generation, stems export

## Boomy
- Free: $0 — limited generations, save songs
- Creator: $9.99/mo — unlimited saves, more generations
- Pro: $29.99/mo — unlimited saves, 25 MP3/WAV downloads/mo, 10 monthly releases to streaming platforms
- Key features: One-click generation, distribution to Spotify/Apple Music, royalty collection, simple interface
